ABOUT US

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Wallet Store began as a small family business with a passion for craftsmanship and quality leather goods. What started as a modest workshop in San Francisco has grown into a renowned brand trusted by customers worldwide.

Our Journey

Our founder, Michael Thompson, learned the art of leatherworking from his grandfather, a master craftsman who created bespoke wallets for over 50 years. Inspired by this rich heritage, Michael set out to combine traditional techniques with modern designs.

In the early days, each wallet was handcrafted in our small workshop. As demand grew, we expanded our team with skilled artisans who share our commitment to quality and attention to detail.

Our Mission

At Wallet Store, we believe that a wallet is more than just an accessory—it's a daily companion that should combine functionality, durability, and style. Our mission is to create premium wallets that stand the test of time, both in quality and design.

Our Commitment

We source only the finest materials, from premium full-grain leather to high-quality hardware. Each wallet undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets our exacting standards before reaching our customers.

Today, we continue to innovate while honoring traditional craftsmanship, creating wallets that our customers are proud to carry every day.
